§ 01 About

Cermain has practised in Hong Kong since 2009, advising founders, funds, regional businesses, universities, and private clients on M&A, venture capital, cross-border transactions, and personal investment matters. Her practice spans seed rounds for first-time founders to multi-billion-yuan acquisitions for HKEX-listed companies — always on the company and founder side. Many of her clients she has acted for over a decade. She co-leads OLN's Startup Tech team, chairs the Women Initiative APEC of GlobaLaw, and lectures part-time in commercial contract drafting at HKU Law School.

"Contracts should be clear, fair, and built for real relationships — not weapons stockpiled for the day the relationship ends."
