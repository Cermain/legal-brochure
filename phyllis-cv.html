§ 01 About

Phyllis is a Hong Kong and England & Wales qualified solicitor whose practice centres on emerging companies, M&A, and venture capital. In five years of practice she has led 10+ acquisitions for listed clients, structured VIE and WFOE vehicles for pre-IPO companies, and advised founders from seed through Series B across AI, fintech, biotech, and proptech. Within OLN's Startup Tech team she leads drafting support on shareholder arrangements and owns the operational document workstream.

"Operational documents are where well-meaning SHAs go to die. The cleanest drafting on earth still needs an Article that bites."
