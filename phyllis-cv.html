§ 01 About

Phyllis is a Hong Kong and England & Wales qualified solicitor with a practice centred on emerging companies, M&A, and venture capital. In five years of practice she has led 10+ acquisitions for listed clients, structured VIE and WFOE vehicles for pre-IPO companies, and advised founders from seed through Series B across AI, fintech, biotech, and property technology.
